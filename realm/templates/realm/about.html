About REALM

What is REALM?

REALM (Research Environment for Applied Learning in Materials) is a platform that integrates materials science ontologies with large language models to create an interactive learning and research environment.

The platform allows researchers, educators, and students to explore materials science concepts through natural language interactions with an AI assistant that has knowledge of materials science domains.

The MSEO Ontology

REALM uses the Materials Science Engineering Ontology (MSEO) from MatPortal.org. MSEO provides a structured knowledge representation of materials science concepts, properties, and relationships.

The ontology includes definitions of:

  • Material classes and types
  • Material properties and characteristics
  • Processing techniques and methods
  • Characterization methods
  • Relationships between materials and their applications

Integration with Large Language Models

REALM uses advanced language models like Claude to provide natural language interactions with the materials science knowledge base. The integration works through:

  1. Model Context Protocol (MCP) Integration: REALM uses the Model Context Protocol to connect LLMs with the ontology data.
  2. Ontology-Enhanced Prompting: The system generates appropriate prompts based on the selected ontology and user queries.
  3. Structured Knowledge Representation: Information from the ontology is transformed into formats that can be effectively utilized by language models.

How to Use REALM

REALM offers several ways to interact with materials science knowledge:

LLM Chat

Ask questions about materials science using natural language.

Materials Explorer

Browse and search for specific materials and their properties.

Project Goal

REALM aims to demonstrate the potential of integrating domain-specific ontologies with large language models to create more accurate, contextually relevant AI assistants for specific knowledge domains. The approach used in this project could be extended to other scientific and technical domains beyond materials science.

  • MSEO ontology from MatPortal.org

Note: REALM is a research project. Information provided should be verified against scientific sources for critical applications.
