How Precedent Ingestion Works
  1. Primary case ingestion: When you add a case via /cases/new, it's marked as a "primary" case.
  2. URL extraction: The system scans the case content for links to other NSPE BER cases.
  3. One-level ingestion: Up to 20 cited cases are automatically ingested as "precedent" cases.
  4. Backlog tracking: Additional cited URLs are stored for later manual ingestion.
  5. No recursion: Precedent cases don't trigger further auto-ingestion (prevents explosion).
