About Experiment Setup

This form allows you to configure the basic parameters for your ProEthica experiment:

Configuration Options

  • Name: A descriptive name for your experiment to help you identify it later.
  • Description: Notes about the purpose, hypothesis, or other details about this experiment run.
  • Leave Out Conclusion: When enabled, the conclusion section of cases will be withheld from the prompts, allowing the system to predict what the conclusion should be.

Next Steps

After creating the experiment, you'll be prompted to select which engineering ethics cases to include in the experiment run.
