About ProEthica Experiments

The ProEthica experiment interface allows you to evaluate engineering ethics cases using different analysis conditions:

Experiment Conditions

  • Baseline Condition: Plain prompt with standard embedding-based retrieval and basic precedent matching
  • ProEthica Enhanced: Multi-metric relevance scoring, ontology-constrained FIRAC prompting, bidirectional validation, and professional framework grounding

Leave-One-Out Procedure

The leave-one-out procedure removes the conclusion section from the case and tasks the system with predicting the decision that would be made by the NSPE Board of Ethical Review.

Evaluation Metrics

Experiments are evaluated based on multiple criteria, including reasoning quality, persuasiveness, coherence, accuracy, and alignment with the original NSPE conclusions.
