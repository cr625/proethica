About the Ethical Decision Wizard

The Ethical Decision Wizard guides you through complex ethical dilemmas using a structured approach:

  1. Situation Analysis: Define the problem and identify stakeholders
  2. Ethical Framework: Apply relevant ethical principles and guidelines
  3. Options Evaluation: Consider possible courses of action
  4. Precedent Review: Examine similar cases and outcomes
  5. Recommendation: Get AI-powered analysis and suggestions
  6. Action Planning: Create concrete next steps

Each session is saved and can be resumed at any time. Completed sessions generate comprehensive reports that can be exported or shared.
